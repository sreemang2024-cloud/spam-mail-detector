The pipeline

Three steps from raw CSV to working filter.

01

Upload a dataset

Drop in any Kaggle spam/ham CSV — SMS Spam Collection, Enron, phishing corpora. Sentinel auto-detects the label and text columns.

02

Train the model

A TF-IDF vectorizer feeds a Logistic Regression or Naive Bayes classifier. Metrics — accuracy, precision, recall, F1 — are computed on a held-out test split.

03

Detect in real time

Paste a single email or upload a batch. Every verdict comes with a confidence score and the exact signals that drove it.

Download a labeled spam dataset from Kaggle (e.g. "SMS Spam Collection Dataset" or "Spam Email Dataset"), then upload the CSV here. Columns named label/text, v1/v2, or category/message are detected automatically.
